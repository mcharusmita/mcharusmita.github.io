About New York City

New York City is known as the city that never sleeps. No matter what time it it, you will always have something to do. You can live in New York city for a complete year and still not be able to visit every place in New York City. As a tourist in New York, tourists only make a point to visit:
  • Empire State
  • MoMa
  • Statue of Liberty
  • Times Square
However there is more to discover in New York City. If you want to get a better idea of what 'The City that never sleeps' is all about, it is a must to visit:
  • Brooklyn
  • Manhattan
  • Queens
